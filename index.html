Why ride with T&K service?

Cabs for Every Pocket

From Sedans and SUVs to Luxury cars for special occasions, we have cabs to suit every pocket

T&K Texi serviece Select

A membership program with T&K that lets you ride a Prime Sedan at Mini fares, book cabs without peak pricing and has zero wait time

Share and Express

To travel with the lowest fares choose T&K Share. For a faster travel experience we have Share Express on some fixed routes with zero deviations.

Secure and Safer Rides

Verified drivers, an emergency alert button, and live ride tracking are some of the features that we have in place to ensure you a safe travel experience.

In Cab Entertainment

Play music, watch videos and a lot more with T&K Play! Also stay connected even if you are travelling through poor network areas with our free wifi facility.

Cashless Rides

Now go cashless and travel easy. Simply recharge your T&K money or add your credit/debit card to enjoy hassle free payments.
